About Me

Perrin Jones

Engineer, Designer, Advocate.

Welcome to my portfolio! I’m a sophomore at Columbia University studying Computer Science & Mechanical Engineering under the engineering school and Sound Arts under the graduate School of the Arts. Combining these fields, I’ve done acoustic based quantum computing research in Australia, robotics design and algorithmic work for Hod Lipson’s Creative Machines Lab, and audio engineering for various professional theatres and Grammy-nominated artists. I'm currently interested in pursuing opportunities in software, UI/UX design, and product design. I hope to continue work in engineering and arts to design and implement a better world for all.
